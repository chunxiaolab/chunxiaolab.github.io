Postdoc Application

Introduction:

  • Peking University Third Hospital, established in 1958, is a comprehensive Class-A tertiary hospital managed by the National Health Commission. It integrates medical treatment, teaching, scientific research, prevention, health care, rehabilitation, and health management. Upholding the hospital's spirit of "Unity, Dedication, Pragmatism, and Innovation," Peking University Third Hospital carries the heavy responsibility of life with superb medical skills and a high sense of responsibility. To build a "National Medical Center," a "High-Level Diagnosis and Treatment Center for Complex and Severe Diseases," a "Clinical Medical Research Center," and a "Medical Talent Training Base," and to meet the talent needs for the hospital's high-quality development and lay a foundation for cultivating high-level interdisciplinary medical talents, the hospital is now recruiting clinical and research postdoctoral researchers according to the relevant regulations of Peking University Health Science Center and Peking University Third Hospital. Outstanding young talents are welcome to join!

I. Recruitment Specializations:

  • Fields: Oncology, Immunology, and Cell Biology, etc.

II. Postdoctoral Benefits:

  • 1. Annual Salary: Starting from 2024, postdoctoral remuneration will be fully enhanced;
  • - General postdoctoral: 280,000 RMB/year;
  • - Selected for the "Peking University Third Hospital Sailing Postdoctoral" program: 330,000 RMB/year;
  • - Selected for the "Peking University Health Science Center Boya Postdoctoral" program: 350,000 RMB/year;
  • - Selected for the "National Postdoctoral Innovative Talent Support Program": 450,000 RMB/year;
  • - Outstanding performance during the station period: an increase of 20,000 RMB in annual salary.
  • (The "Sailing Postdoctoral" program and the "Peking University Health Science Center Boya Postdoctoral" program are scheduled to be released in March 2024, with specific application information to be announced accordingly.)
  • 2. Performance: Enjoy hospital scientific research rewards, department and research group performance rewards.
  • 3. Housing: Eligible to apply for postdoctoral apartments provided by the Health Science Center and the hospital.
  • 4. Household Registration: Non-Beijing household registration can be transferred to Beijing, and spouses and children can move according to regulations.
  • 5. Insurance: Enjoy public medical treatment, and pay social insurance, housing provident fund, etc.
  • 6. Overseas: Eligible to apply for government-sponsored overseas exchange and study.
  • 7. Employment: Outstanding candidates will be given priority for hospital employment.

III. Selection Criteria:

  • 1. Graduates with a relevant doctoral degree, having obtained the degree within the past 3 years, and under 35 years old;
  • 2. Strong clinical, research, innovation, and teamwork abilities, with at least one paper published or formally accepted in a SCI-indexed journal as the first author during the doctoral period;
  • 3. Clinical postdoctoral candidates must have obtained a practicing physician qualification certificate and have more than one year of standardized residency training experience in the relevant specialty;
  • 4. Candidates who meet the above conditions will be selected on a competitive basis.

IV. Recruitment Procedures:

  • 1. Postdoctoral admission times are in January, April, August, and October each year, and candidates can apply at any time;
  • 2. Applicants should contact their intended collaborating supervisor, send a personal resume and research achievements (supervisor's email can be found in the collaborating supervisor's profile), and the supervisor will organize an interview;
  • 3. The hospital organizes interviews in March, June, September, and December each year. Candidates who pass the supervisor's interview and meet the requirements can participate in the hospital interview. Clinical postdoctoral candidates must participate in the clinical skills assessment organized by the hospital;
  • 4. Candidates who pass the hospital interview, pass the entry medical examination, and have no objections during the public announcement will complete the admission and entry procedures as required.

三、选拔条件:

  • 1、相关专业博士毕业生，获得博士学位不超过3年，年龄不超过35周岁；
  • 2、具有较强的临床、科研、创新及团队协作能力，博士期间以第一作者身份在SCI收录期刊上发表或被正式接收论著1篇；
  • 3、临床型博士后要求已获得执业医师资格证书，且具有本专业一年以上的住院医师规范化培训经历；

四、招收程序:

  • 1、博士后进站时间为每年1月、4月、8月、10月，候选人可随时申请；
  • 2、申请者与意向合作导师联系，发送个人简历、已取得研究成果（导师邮箱见合作导师简介），合作导师组织面试；
  • 3、医院于每年3、6、9、12月组织医院面试，通过合作导师面试、符合条件的申请人可以参加医院面试，临床型博士后需参加医院组织的临床技能考核；
  • 4、医院面试通过，入职体检合格，公示无疑议的申请人，按要求办理进站、入职手续。
